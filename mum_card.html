For my Mum, with all my love

Thank you for being the quiet strength behind every one of my steps. For the meals, the midnight check-ins, the brave face when I was scared, and the way you always made room for my dreams—even when it meant putting yours on hold.

You taught me kindness that isn’t loud, courage that isn’t showy, and love that doesn’t keep score. I notice the little things now—the texts, the reminders, the hugs that last a second longer—and I carry them with me every day.

If I could gift you anything, it would be the way you make me feel: safe, seen, and home. Until then, please take this small promise: I’ll keep trying to be the kind of person you raised me to be. Always. ❤
